About EcoGarden

Founded in 2015, EcoGarden began with a simple mission: to create beautiful outdoor spaces that work in harmony with nature rather than against it.

Our team of certified horticulturists and landscape designers combine aesthetic vision with ecological science to deliver gardens that are sustainable, low-maintenance, and beneficial to local wildlife.
